Register and Check-In

Please register for the conference, if you haven't done so. It helps us to have a complete list. Use the code fr2022speaker

https://frontrunners.ticketleap.com/frontrunners2022/

Please check in with the registration desk when you arrive and tell them you are a speaker, so we know you are at the venue. And say hello to all of the organizers, especially if we have not actually met you in person before! We want to meet you.

Video Adapters

Please bring your HDMI video adapter (your laptop to HDMI) if you need one. The venue uses HDMI. I have an adapter from USB-C to HDMI, but adapters sometimes get lost and we are left looking for one at the last minute.

Try the Projection

Please try your presentation as soon as you can after you get there, either in the morning or a break. Make sure you have the mirroring, resolution, etc. set up the way you want before your talk. It is much better to have those issues solved before your talk begins! There will be power at the podium. The venue will be open to us starting at 7 AM. We will definately be there by 8 AM.

Wifi

Wifi will be available, but as always it might not work! Please do not depend upon it. Have a version of your presentation on your laptop. You will need to get a wireless account there, I believe. I am checking on that now.

The projection is best at 16:9 or 4:3 aspect ratio, but 16:9 will look better.

Please avoid small text on your slides.

Speaker T-Shirts

Let us know your t-shirt sizes so we can save you a shirt. t-shirt size form!

Schedule

Please try to keep your talk to its scheduled length, and please start at your scheduled time. This will give some time for the following speaker to set up, and not go into the break times.

The schedule is here:

https://frontrunners.tech/#schedule

Backup Talks

As sometimes happens, we have one speaker who might have jury duty on the day of Frontrunners. We won't know the outcome of that until the last minute, unfortunately. So, if you have a second talk, and are willing to give that as a backup, we would appreciate it.

Lunch

The conference does not provide lunch. There are so many lunch places nearby that when we have done that in the past, everyone goes out anyway! But we want to pay for speaker lunches. Please get a $20 bill from the registration desk or any of the organizers for that before you go out (or more if you need it, especially if you can give us a receipt).
