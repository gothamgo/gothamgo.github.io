Video Adapters

The venue will have an HDMI connection at the podium. Please bring your HDMI video adapter (your laptop to HDMI) if you need one. We have some adapters to HDMI, but adapters sometimes get lost and we are left looking for one at the last minute.

Try the Projection

Please try your presentation as soon as you can after you get there, either in the morning or a break. Make sure you have the mirroring, resolution, etc. set up the way you want before your talk. It is much better to have those issues solved before your talk begins! There will be power at the podium.

The projection is best at 16:9 or 4:3 aspect ratio, but 16:9 will look better.

Please avoid small text on your slides.

Wifi

Wifi access will be poor! Please do not depend upon it. Have a version of your presentation that does not depend upn the internet on your laptop.

Schedule

Please try to keep your talk to its scheduled length, and please start at your scheduled time. This will give some time for the following speaker to set up, and not go into the break times.

The schedule is here — Schedule. Let us know if you need to move your times.

Backup Talks

We will try to arrange for a back up speaker, in case someone can't be there at the last minute. But if you have a second talk, and are willing to give that as a backup if we need it, we would appreciate hearing about it.
